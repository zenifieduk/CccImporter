About The Classic Car Clubs Directory

Welcome to the UK's premier directory of classic car clubs. Our mission is to connect enthusiasts with local and national clubs dedicated to preserving and celebrating classic automobiles.

Whether you're looking to join a club for your specific make or model, or simply want to connect with fellow classic car enthusiasts in your area, our comprehensive directory has you covered.

From vintage British marques to American muscle, European sports cars to Japanese classics, you'll find clubs catering to every interest and era of motoring history.
